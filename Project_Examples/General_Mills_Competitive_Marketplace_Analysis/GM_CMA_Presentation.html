General Mills Competitive Marketplace Analysis

  • Goal of analysis

    • Examine the performance of General Mills promotion and advertisement strategies
    • Compare against two main competitors: Kelloggs and Post
    • Sales performance from 15 different cereals
    • Promotion variance between brands and impact sales performance
    • Advertisement use and measure sales impact

  • Data and approach

    • ~187,000 unique sales
    • 15 unique cereal products (5 General Mills, 8 Kelloggs, 2 Post)
    • On promotion vs no promotion
    • in-store Advertisement of two sizes; small and medium
    • Across ~20,000 stores

  • Findings

    • General Mills outperforms both Kelloggs and Post in average weekly revenue under promotions and advertisements
    • Cocoa Puffs performs best with a promotion and small advertisement

General Mills promotion and advertisement performance comparison

  • General Mills outperforms both Kelloggs and Post in average weekly revenue under promotions and advertisements

    • Top performing scenario is a promotion combined with medium advertisement
    • Followed by promotion only
    • Lowest performing is with products at full MSRP
    • Only beat by Kelloggs when running promotion and small advertisement
    • Promotion with small ad and both stand alone advertisement campaigns perform similarly

Cocoa Puff Performance

  • Cocoa Puffs performs best with a promotion and small advertisement

    • Typically a lower performing cereal
    • Performance with a promotion and small ad jumps considerably over MSRP (Almost 2x)
    • individual advertisement performance is below full MSRP

Recommendations

  1. General Mills current strategies are performing well compared to the competition

    • Recommend focusing on promotions alone or with medium advertisements
    • Depending on advertisement costs the additional revenue boost may or may not be cost effective over exclusive promotion use
    • Less focus on promotions with small advertisements overall

  1. Increase promotion frequency for Cocoa Puffs

    • Recommend increasing promotion with medium and frequency in this specific case
    • Less focus on medium and small ad strategies due to low performance
